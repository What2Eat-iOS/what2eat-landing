Our Story

A simple solution to a complex problem: understanding what's in our food

The Problem

Food labels are confusing. Hidden ingredients, complex nutritional information, and unclear health impacts make it difficult to make informed choices about what we eat.

The Solution

What2Eat simplifies food labels. Scan any product to instantly understand its ingredients, nutritional value, and health impact, helping you make better food choices.

The Team

As student developers from SRM University, Himavarshith Reddy, Sumanaswi Akkisetty, and Maneeth Reddy developed What2Eat to make healthy eating simpler for everyone.

The Mission

We're making food transparency accessible to everyone. Whether you're a parent, health-conscious individual, or simply curious about what's in your food, What2Eat helps you make informed choices.
